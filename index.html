NATIONAL EDUCATION SUMMIT
2023

Join principals, school leaders and educators from K-12 at the National Education Summit. Held over two days educators can experience innovative Conferences and interactive Seminars delivered by experts and thought leaders from the education sector. Educators can also engage with exhibitors showcasing the latest in resources at the The Education Show, an education focused Free Trade Expo.
